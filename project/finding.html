Needfinding

I have researched about 20 k-pop promoting websites and realized that the most of websites have not been updated and look like something built in 2007. Moreover, I saw a lot of material design and IOS design are used repetitively for no reason. I decided to keep my website simple and stick to just one purpose I proposed in the beginning. I have conducted an interview and figured out that current k-pop webistes are too overwhelming and have too much info on main pages. Therefore, users could not decide what to do and those websites lose customer traffic.
My website needs to be able to responsive and optimized. My website needs to be able to have SEO meta tags. My website needs to be able to work like a single application website. My website needs to be able to change its background color randomly. My website needs to be able to impress people with its fast and optimized videos My website needs to be able to help new users with built in robochat My website needs to be able to make animations and transition to be consistant. My website needs to be minimal and have less info than other k-pop websites.
